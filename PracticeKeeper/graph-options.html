Which Data Should Be Included?

The current site is a work in progress so there are some buttons may not do anything or respond strangely, but the idea of the project is for me to keep track of my practice habits throughout my time at the University of Ottawa studying Tenor Trombone. This project was an idea that came to me one day while walking home and wondering why there are no apps/ websites that help you visualize and track your practice habits. By manipulating and analyzing data I hope to bring new insights to my own practice to help me better utilize the time I spend and eventually help others do the same. As with many things in life it is important to note that practicing more does not make you better. The musician that spends 2 hours of focused and thoughtful practice is often more successful than the musician who approaches their craft with a brute force and repetition as a means to improve and hone their skills. This site will be updated as a find the time to do so to make it run better, and be more user friendly. Please feel free to email me with any features you'd like to see changed, added or even removed. My email is daltonmooring@gmail.com; enjoy.
